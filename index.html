Types of arduino

Arduino Uno

It is the most common used arduino for projects,due to it's satisfactory storage and medium size.

Arduino Nano

It is a small version of arduino, and is used for making projects that are less in size and of low weight.

Arduino Mega

It is the largest version of arduino, and is used for large projects that require a lot of storage too.

Arduino Micro

It is one of the smallest versions of arduino, and is used in very small type of projects.

I have told about the various arduino's but i will only use arduino uno for my projects
